The Silent Epidemic

Current Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) technology has stagnated, failing millions with tools that are slow, impersonal, and prohibitively expensive. This isn't just a technical failure; it's a public health crisis that fuels social isolation and diminishes quality of life.

The Scope of the Challenge

A significant portion of individuals with common neurological conditions face profound communication challenges. For instance, within the large population diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), a substantial number remain minimally verbal for life.

Source: CDC, Communication interventions for autism spectrum disorder in minimally verbal children - PMC.

The Debilitating Latency

The gap between the speed of natural conversation and current AAC tools is vast. This delay makes fluid, real-time interaction nearly impossible, leading to frustration and disconnection.

Source: External challenges for individuals who need or use AAC - Taylor & Francis Online.

Introducing COGGI

COGGI is Capgemini's proposed paradigm shift. It's not just another AAC app; it's a personalized, AI-powered communication partner designed for speed, emotional resonance, and deep user engagement.

Predictive & Fast

Moves beyond word prediction to anticipate user intent, suggesting full, context-aware phrases to enable communication at near-conversational speed.

Emotionally Resonant

Integrates emotionally expressive voice synthesis and non-verbal cues, allowing users to convey not just what they think, but how they feel.

Deeply Personal

Features customizable "COGGI Companion" avatars (animals, characters) to foster user engagement, ownership, and a positive interactive experience.

The Core Innovation: The Digital Twin

The technology that elevates COGGI is the Digital Twin of the User (DToU). It's a dynamic, learning model of the user's entire communicative identity. It learns not just words, but personality, relationships, and context to provide truly authentic communication support.

How It Works: Hybrid Architecture

COGGI's performance and security are built on a robust Hybrid Cloud-Edge Architecture. This model balances real-time responsiveness on the user's device with powerful personalization in the cloud, ensuring a seamless, secure, and always-available experience.

1. On-Device (Edge)

Real-time prediction and user interaction happens locally on the smartphone or tablet. This guarantees low latency and offline functionality.

Secure Sync

2. HIPAA-Compliant Cloud

The heavy lifting—training the DToU and fine-tuning AI models—is offloaded to a secure cloud backend, ensuring user data privacy and security.

The Path to Impact

Capgemini's strategy provides a credible, de-risked path from a validated prototype to a sustainable and accessible product. This is built on a deep understanding of the market, regulatory pathways, and our intrinsic capabilities as a global technology leader.

Market Opportunity

The AAC market is poised for significant expansion, growing from ~$2B to an estimated $5.2B by the early 2030s.

Regulatory Pathway

COGGI is positioned as a Class II Medical Device (SaMD), following a well-established 510(k) pathway using product code ILQ. This clear path minimizes regulatory risk.

FDA Class II 510(k) Pathway

Reimbursement Strategy

To ensure accessibility, our strategy targets coverage under the existing HCPCS code E2511 for "Speech generating software," providing a direct route to reimbursement.
